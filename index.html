COCO MADEMOISELLE

PRODUCT

Irresistibly sensual, irrepressibly spirited. A sparkling, bold floral-woody fragrance that recalls a daring young Coco Chanel. An absolutely modern composition with a strong yet surprisingly fresh character.

Sparks of fresh and vibrant orange immediately awaken the senses. A clear and sensual heart reveals the transparent accords of jasmine and May rose. The scent finally unfurls with the vibrant accents of patchouli and vetiver.

PARIS-ÉDIMBOURG

PRODUCT

An aromatic, woody fragrance that conjures the wild and majestic landscape of the Scottish Highlands, where Gabrielle Chanel found solace in nature.

SA powerful burst of juniper berry and cypress evolves into a warm vetiver accord with earthy undertones and smoky accents, for a warm-yet-surprisingly-fresh scent. 

EAU DE COLOGNE

PRODUCT

An iconic symbol of elegance and simplicity through the ages, this light and luminous fragrance evokes a bouquet of wildflowers.

Composed by Jacques Polge—Creator of CHANEL Fragrance from 1978 to 2014—this exquisite interpretation reveals the lively freshness of mandarin and bergamot with airy notes of neroli.

SEOUL

PRODUCT

Intimate, seductive and intensely brilliant. ZARA SOUL is a contemporary expression of magnetic sensuality told in a modern, luminous Oriental fragrance for women.

Sparks of fresh and vibrant orange immediately awaken the senses. A clear and sensual heart reveals the transparent accords of jasmine and May rose. The scent finally unfurls with the vibrant accents of patchouli and vetiver.
